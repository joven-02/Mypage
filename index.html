ABOUT US

   JV & M ASSOCIATES, INCORPORATED is a duly registered and licensed construction company with architectural and engineering capability in most types of construction undertakings.

   JV &M ASSOCIATES, INCORPORATED is organized as a manpower and service contractor for both construction and maintenance projects.

   The company naturally existed as a corporation and is composed of a group of young and talented professionals and managers with an aggregate construction experience of more than 30 years. This collective experience stretches its capabilities over a wide range of projects types, ranging from interior renovation, building repair and construction of commercial to industrial buildings.

   JV&M ASSOCIATES, INCORPORATED was incorporated on February 2001, but behind it is very rich track record in construction industry, Its capability covers the whole range of architectural, civil, steel fabrication, electrical and mechanical works.
